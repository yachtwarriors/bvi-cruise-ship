# BVI/USVI Online Community Research
*April 2026*

## Facebook Groups (Primary Target)

### BVI-Focused
- **BVI Travel Tips & Recommendations** (facebook.com/groups/1266848426813237) -- The group ranking in SERPs for "bvi cruise ship schedule." Members actively post questions like "How can you find out the cruise ship schedule to the Baths?" Direct match for the tool.
- **BVI Community/Travel Group** (facebook.com/groups/1399241897182431) -- Someone literally posted recommending bvicruiseshipschedule.com here already.

### USVI-Focused
- **What's Going on St. Thomas?** (facebook.com/groups/241455502718865) -- General St. Thomas community group. Ranking in SERPs.
- **St. Thomas Travel & Tips** (facebook.com/groups/893397532400813) -- Ranking for "st thomas cruise ship schedule." A post literally asks "Cruise ship schedule for St Thomas port?"
- **ST JOHN USVI TRAVEL & LIFE** (facebook.com/groups/stjohntravelandlife2.0) -- Active St. John travel group. Cruise crowds impact St. John heavily (20K+ cruise pax on busy St. Thomas days overflow to Cruz Bay/Trunk Bay).
- **What's Going on St. John** (facebook.com/groups/1850511531842592) -- Another St. John community group.

## Cruise Critic Forums (High Intent)

### Tortola Forum
- **boards.cruisecritic.com/forum/202-tortola/** -- Dedicated Tortola subforum. Active threads about avoiding cruise crowds, what to do on port days, walkability from cruise pier. People asking exactly the questions our tool answers.
- Key threads: "Timing things to avoid cruise ship crowds," "Avoiding cruise crowds," "Why so much panic over cruise ships?"

### St. Thomas/St. John Forum
- **boards.cruisecritic.com** St. Thomas/St. John section -- Very active. Multiple threads about port logistics, crowd levels, docking schedules.

## Reddit (Lower Volume, Still Relevant)

- **r/Cruise** -- General cruise subreddit. BVI/USVI port questions come up but not dominant.
- **r/USVirginIslands** -- Small but relevant subreddit for USVI travelers.
- **r/sailing** / **r/travel** -- Occasional BVI threads but low volume.
- Reddit is the weakest channel for this niche compared to Facebook groups and forums.

## Priority Order for Outreach

1. **Facebook Groups** -- Highest ROI. Posts ranking in Google, people asking exact questions the tool answers, and organic sharing potential. The 4 USVI + 2 BVI groups are the core targets.
2. **Cruise Critic Tortola & St. Thomas forums** -- High-intent cruisers actively planning port days. Answer threads with helpful context + link.
3. **TripAdvisor Tortola & St. Thomas forums** -- Same audience, slightly less active than Cruise Critic for cruise-specific questions.
4. **Reddit** -- Lowest priority. Smaller audience for this niche, and Reddit is hostile to self-promotion.
