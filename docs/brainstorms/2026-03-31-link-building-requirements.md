# Link Building Strategy for bvicruiseshipschedule.com

## Problem Frame

BVI Cruise Ship Schedule is a better tool than anything currently ranking for BVI and USVI cruise schedule keywords, but the site is new and not yet in Ahrefs' index. User reports ranking on page 2 in Google. The #1 result for "bvi cruise ship schedule" (bviports.org/cruise) is a **dead 404 page**, creating an immediate opportunity. The site needs backlinks to climb into the top 3 for BVI keywords and crack the top 5 for the higher-volume USVI keywords.

## Requirements

- R1. Produce a prioritized outreach target list with sites that currently link to inferior or broken BVI/USVI cruise schedule tools
- R2. Identify and document all broken link opportunities on bviports.org (the /cruise page is a confirmed 404)
- R3. Create outreach email templates for broken link replacement and better-tool-replacement pitches
- R4. Build a phased action plan (4 weeks) starting with highest-conversion opportunities
- R5. Document the competitive SERP landscape and keyword volumes for future reference

## Success Criteria

- Outreach list of 15+ actionable targets with specific URLs, contact angles, and what they currently link to
- At least 5 backlinks acquired within 60 days
- Ranking improvement from page 2 to top 5 for "bvi cruise ship schedule" (150 vol/mo)
- First page appearance for "st thomas cruise ship schedule" (800 vol/mo) within 90 days

## Scope Boundaries

- No new content pages or site changes in this effort — link building only
- No paid link acquisition
- No automated outreach tools — manual, personalized emails
- Forum/community participation is in scope but should be genuine, not spammy

## Key Decisions

- **Focus on link building only:** Content pages (port guides, beach pages) deferred to a future effort despite high-volume keyword opportunities like "tortola cruise port" (1,600 vol/mo, KD 0)
- **Research doc format:** Full strategy doc saved to `docs/link-building-strategy.md` with tiered target list, templates, and action plan
- **Broken link angle is primary:** bviports.org/cruise being a 404 while ranking #1 is the strongest outreach angle

## Dependencies / Assumptions

- The broken government link (bviports.org/cruise = 404) remains broken during the outreach period
- Site continues to be indexed and crawled by Google
- Crowd prediction feature remains a unique differentiator vs. competitors

Full research and strategy document: `docs/link-building-strategy.md`
