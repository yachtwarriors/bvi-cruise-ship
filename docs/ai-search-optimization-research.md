# AI Search / GEO Optimization Research (April 2026)

## How AI Assistants Pick Sources for Niche Queries

- **Perplexity** favors blog-style articles (46% of citations) and mainstream news (20%), plus community content from Reddit/forums (4%). It extracts at sentence level, picking the most quotable statement per section.
- **ChatGPT** favors encyclopedic, data-dense content.
- **Google AI Overviews** leans on Wikipedia (27%) and existing top-ranking content.
- **Data-rich sites generate 4.31x more citations per URL** than directory listings.
- Adding statistics improves AI visibility by 41% -- the single most effective technique.
- Perplexity cites original research/data in 73% of business-related answers.
- Niche authority beats broad authority: a niche site cited 30 times outperforms a famous publication that never appears for your prompts.

## What Gets You Cited (Actionable)

1. **Direct answers in first 40-60 words** of each section
2. **Stat density**: one statistic every 150-200 words
3. **FAQPage schema**: 30% improvement in AI citation rates; 2.5x higher chance of appearing in AI answers with proper schema
4. **Article + Organization schema** in JSON-LD format
5. **Content freshness**: recent "Last Modified" dates beat stale content every time
6. **Allow crawlers**: PerplexityBot must be allowed in robots.txt
7. **Author attribution**: explicit bylines improve citation probability
8. **Brand mentions** correlate with AI Overview visibility more than backlinks (r=0.664 per Ahrefs)

## Schema Markup Priority

- JSON-LD is the standard; sits separate from HTML
- FAQPage schema = highest citation rate in AI answers
- Article schema with dateModified
- Organization schema with author info
- Sites with schema are 3.2-3.4x more likely to be cited

## Content Types That Drive Citations

- 32.5% of all LLM citations come from comparative listicles
- Comparison pages, best-of lists, migration guides work well
- Glossary/definition content = zero click-through (avoid)
- Original data/tools > generic content articles

## Channel Importance vs Traditional SEO

- AI-referred sessions jumped 527% YoY in early 2025
- Perplexity referral traffic converts at 10.5% vs 1.76% for Google organic
- New optimized content can appear in Perplexity citations within hours, not months
- This channel is growing fast but still smaller than traditional search volume
